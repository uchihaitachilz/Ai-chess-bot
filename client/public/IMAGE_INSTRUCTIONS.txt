HOW TO ADD TUSHI'S PHOTO:

1. Place the image file in this folder (client/public/)
2. Name it one of these:
   - tushi.jpg
   - tushi.png
   - tushi.jpeg
   - tushi.JPG
   - tushi.PNG
   - tushi.JPEG

3. The image will automatically appear in the commentary panel!

If you have the image file, just copy it here with one of those names.
